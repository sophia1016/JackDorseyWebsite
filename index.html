Jack Dorsey

Jack Dorsey is one of the web developers behind the apps Twitter and Square.

How the named Twitter was picked

The name Twitter came from when the developers of Twitter where trying to come up with a name for the app. They went through the dictionary and found the word Twitter which means, "to talk in a quick and informal way about unimportant things". This was chosen because they knew Twitter would be just short bursts of information people would share. They then chose the bird to represent the app because the sounds birds make can also be defined by the word "twitter".

The term "Tweet"

Surprisingly, the people who used Twitter were the ones who came up with the word "Tweet". It had nothing to do with the people who developed the social media site.
